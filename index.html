About EUCompatibleMemes.com

In the light of the new EU legislation Article 13 I've decided to create this service to illustrate how our everyday memes will look like.

If you don't want to read all of it, this is sums up the jist of it.

Put somewhat extremely; If you take a screenshot of a funny scene in the new Marvel movie, and you add some out of context sentences and upload it to reddit, you are breaching copyright law, but reddit is not to blame, they just share a service.

With the new law from the EU, reddit is just as much to blame, and need to protect themselves from lawsuits by filtering and censoring content, creating the memeworld as we know it to an end.

This service is, again, put somewhat extremely, is demonstrating what memes will have to look like if they are to survive copyright identification software.
